With Chicken, Pork Skin, and Potato

WHOLESOME SNACKS FOR DOGS

Lots of goodies packed on an edible skewer will keep your dog busy and happy. Kabobs are full of wholesome chicken, pork skin, and potato.

Ingredients:

White meat chicken, pork skin, potato, wheat flour, soy flour, vegetable glycerin, rice flour, preserved with mixed-tocopherols (form of Vitamin E).

Feeding Guidelines:

Small Dogs 5 - 10 lbs (2.3 – 4.5 kg) 1 treat
Small-Medium Dogs 11 – 25 lbs (5.0 – 11.3 kg) 1 - 2 treats
Medium Dogs 26 – 50 lbs (11.8 – 22.7 kg) 2 - 3 treats
Large Dogs Over 50 lbs (22.7+ kg) 3 - 4 treats
