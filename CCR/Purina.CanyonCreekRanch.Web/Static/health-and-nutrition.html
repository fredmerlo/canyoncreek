HEALTHY PET FOOD

Every recipe includes high-quality sources of protein, whole grains, antioxidants, and omega-rich ingredients to help support a healthy lifestyle. We expect the best for our dogs and cats, so we use natural ingredients and never add artificial colors, flavors, or preservatives. Because they cause allergies in some dogs, our formulas contain no corn, no wheat, and no soy. Natural, healthy pet food is simply what our dogs and cats need.

WHOLESOME PET SNACKS

Snack time is a reward, a celebration, and a together time that brings us closer to our dogs. It’s such a special moment; we want to ensure we’re giving our dogs the best snacks with the most wholesome ingredients possible.

We use simple, natural ingredients, never adding artificial flavors or preservatives. Offering a variety of snacks in fun shapes and sizes with real ingredients is what we want for our dogs. Savory, slow-cooked chicken breast, flavorful baked chews, slices of apple and banana wrapped with chicken breast filets, and crunchy chicken and rice bites are just a part of our wide range of snacks. There are three words that mean everything to our snacks– wholesome, tasty, and fun.

Every day we work to ensure product quality and safety and we stand behind every Canyon Creek Ranch product. Below are our standards for maintaining the integrity and safety of our food and snacks.

All Canyon Creek Ranch cat food, dog food, and dog snacks are manufactured at certified facilities inspected regularly by our own Quality Assurance team and third party independent auditors.

Canyon Creek Ranch uses only high-quality ingredients in its products, and the production facilities are designed and operated to meet USDA standards.

We have a dedicated team of quality control experts in the plants when the products are being produced. They monitor various steps of the manufacturing process for safety and quality of the product.

Our quality control group seeks to monitor the good manufacturing practices of our suppliers. Our safety inspection program has specific protocols and practices to help ensure proper food handling sanitation practices, cooking procedures and traceability of all lots back to the raw ingredients.

Canyon Creek Ranch has a comprehensive food safety program in place to ensure the safety of our products.

We maintain a strict code of standards for buying, storing and processing ingredients. These standards are among the strictest, if not the strictest, in the pet food industry.

We have a very rigorous evaluation and sampling program in place for all raw materials and have quality assurance specialists at each of our North American pet food manufacturing facilities that are trained to sample and/or analyze incoming ingredients.

HOLISTIC LIVING WITH YOUR PET

At Canyon Creek Ranch, we believe in the power of holistic living. To make sure each day is enjoyed with our pets, we give them they need to thrive: boundless love, thoughtful care and healthy pet food.

Big gestures aren’t our speed. Neither are flashy gimmicks. Keeping it real and simple. That’s where we find true balance. Here are just a few tips we like:

  • Stress melts in the eyes of your best friend. Take a moment to bond nose-to-nose.
  • While petting your pet, focus completely on them, especially if you’ve had a rough day. You’ll both feel better instantly.
  • Revel in your cat’s antics. A good laugh goes a long way.
  • If you groom your dog, don’t think of it as a chore. Let it be a relaxing, meditative time.
  • When you take your dog (or cat!) for a walk, stand up straight and breathe deeply. Use the time to reenergize.
  • Let playtime with your cat become a time to stretch both of your imaginations. Invent new games with the same toys, or plant new surprises for her to 'hunt' around the house.
  • Variety isn’t just good for your pet. Make a date with your pet once a month, where you try something new like a new park or new toy.
  • Connect with like-minded pet owners. Being part of something so genuine feels good.
